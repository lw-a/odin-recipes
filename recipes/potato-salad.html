Potato Salad

Description

Cold potatoes in a bowl.

It also has eggs and other things

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Boiled eggs
  • Any vegetables you like
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes
  2. Mix mayo and vinegar in a bowl
  3. Mix in the vegetables and eggs too
  4. Add the boiled potatoes
  5. Mix it all together
