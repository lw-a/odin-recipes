Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

What is posted here will easily serve eight people.

Ingredients

  • Enough lasagna sheets
  • Plenty of pasta sauce
  • Mince meat
  • Hearty amount of cheese
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna sheets
  2. Cook mince meat
  3. Add sauce to mince meat
  4. Assemble the lasagna and put cheese on top
  5. Cook it in the oven
