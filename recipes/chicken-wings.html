Baked Chicken Wings

Description

Delicious baked chicken wings fun for the whole family.

What you do with the rest of the chicken is up to you

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Melt Butter
  2. Mix wings with melted butter
  3. Sprinkle seasonings on top
  4. Bake in the oven
  5. Enjoy with your family
